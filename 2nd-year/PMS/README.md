# FAST-TUB: Ticketing and Fleet Management System 🚍

Academic project developed for the **Processos e Metodologias de Software (PMS)** (Software Processes and Methodologies) course at the University of Minho (2025/2026).

### 📋 Context & Description
The local transport authority (Transportes Urbanos de Braga - TUB) is transitioning towards "Mobility 4.0" with the introduction of a new Bus Rapid Transit (BRT) system. 

This repository documents **Subproject 1 - Inception Phase** of the FAST-TUB project, focusing on the Ticketing and Fleet Management System (*Sistema de Ajuda à Exploração - SAE*). The main goal is to design an integrated solution that provides real-time fleet tracking, digital ticketing management, operational/inspection support, and live public information (ETAs and alerts).

### ⚙️ Methodologies & Frameworks
To ensure rigorous planning, quality assurance, and structured development, the project was guided by industry-standard frameworks:
* **OpenUP (Open Unified Process):** Adoption of an iterative and incremental lifecycle with a strict focus on the *Inception Phase* to align stakeholder expectations and validate technical/financial feasibility.
* **CMMi Level 2 (Capability Maturity Model Integration):** Implementation of quality practices ensuring that processes (Requirements Management, Project Planning, Monitoring, etc.) are consistently controlled.
* **PMBOK:** Utilized for top-down project planning, defining scope, schedule, costs, and risk management.

### ✨ Key Features (Use Cases)
The system's architecture was structured around four main functional blocks:
* **Ticketing Management:** Ticket validation, balance top-ups, digital ticket issuance, and integrated purchasing.
* **Inspection & Logging:** On-board ticket validation by inspectors, incident logging, and infraction history tracking.
* **Fleet Tracking & Management:** Real-time GPS fleet monitoring, route deviation detection, and Central Control management.
* **Public Information:** Broadcasting arrival forecasts, delay alerts, and route interruptions to the mobile App and smart bus stops.

### 🛠️ Tools & Technologies
* **SystemStar (COCOMO II):** Used to estimate development effort, duration, and financial costs.
* **ProjectLibre:** Utilized for project planning, WBS (Work Breakdown Structure) creation, Gantt Charts, and PERT/CPM networks.
* **Draw.io:** For technical diagrams and UML system modeling.
* **Canva:** Used to design the UI for the Proof of Concept (PoC), simulating both the Driver's Terminal and the Central Control dashboard.
